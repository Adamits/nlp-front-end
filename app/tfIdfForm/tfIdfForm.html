What the heck is this?

This is a simple app to interface with an API that implements a tf-idf algorithm whose goal is to extract the most relevant, or important terms from a text document. You can paste your text into the form, provide a title, and optionally choose a category that you think best represents your document. After submitting, you will receive a list of the n most important terms.

Check this page out for an explanation of how it works, and what happens to your docuent.
